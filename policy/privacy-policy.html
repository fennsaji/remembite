Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 8, 2026  ·  Effective: March 8, 2026

Overview

Remembite ("we", "our", or "us") is a dish-level intelligence platform that helps you track and discover food you love. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

We do not sell your personal data to third parties. Ever.

Information We Collect

Account information — when you sign in with Google, we receive your name, email address, and profile picture from Google.

Usage data — restaurant visits, dish reactions (Loved it / Tasty / Meh), ratings, favorites, and notes you add.

Location data — approximate location used to find nearby restaurants. We do not store your precise location history.

Device information — device type, OS version, and app version for crash reporting and compatibility.

Payment information — subscription purchases are handled by Google Play. We receive confirmation of your subscription status but never your payment details.

How We Use Your Information

  • Build and improve your personal taste profile
  • Provide dish recommendations and compatibility scores
  • Show nearby restaurants and community dish ratings
  • Send push notifications about your visits and discoveries (only if enabled)
  • Process and verify subscription purchases
  • Diagnose bugs and improve app performance

Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored on secure servers. Data in transit is encrypted using TLS. We use industry-standard practices to protect your information from unauthorised access.

Reaction and visit data is stored locally on your device (offline-first) and optionally synced to our servers for Pro subscribers.

Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Google Sign-In — authentication
  • Google Play Billing — subscription management
  • Google Maps — restaurant location display
  • Firebase (Google) — push notifications
  • Google Gemini — AI-powered dish classification
  • Cloudflare R2 — image storage

Each service has its own privacy policy governing how they handle data.

Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, your personal data is deleted within 30 days. Anonymised, aggregated community data (dish attribute votes, ratings) may be retained to maintain service quality.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your account and associated data
  • Export your reaction and visit history
  • Opt out of push notifications at any time

To exercise these rights, contact us at privacy@remembite.com.

Children's Privacy

Remembite is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have inadvertently collected such information, please contact us immediately.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes via the app or email. Continued use of Remembite after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
